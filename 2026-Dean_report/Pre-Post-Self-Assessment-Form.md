## Rating Scale

Use this scale for **all ratings** in this form. Rate where you are **right now**, not where you hope to be or where you were before.

| Score | Label | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| 4 — Strong | I do this confidently and consistently | I could teach this skill to a peer; I apply it without prompting |
| 3 — Developing | I can do this with some effort | I know how, but I still need to think about it carefully |
| 2 — Early | I have some awareness but limited practice | I understand the concept but struggle to apply it in real situations |
| 1 — Not Yet | I have little to no experience with this | This is new territory for me; I would not know where to start |

## Notes for Administrators

### Instrument Family — How the Three Forms Work Together

| Instrument | File | Who rates | When | Dashboard field |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| This form (pre) | `Pre-Post-Self-Assessment-Form.md` | Student (self) | Week 1 | `Pre_Assessment_Score` |
| This form (post) | `Pre-Post-Self-Assessment-Form.md` | Student (self) | Week 12 | `Post_Assessment_Score` |
| Mini Interview (`20260329-Focus-Group-Interview-Template.md`) | Team Lead | Final 2 weeks | Qualitative narrative, Dean's Report |
| Advanced Interview (`Focus-Group-Interview-Template.md`) | PM/Team Lead (observer) | Final 2 weeks | `PM_Evaluation_Score` (AACSB threshold) |

### SharePoint Columns Required (Student Roster list)

| Column Name | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `Pre_Assessment_Score` | Number (decimal) | Average of LO1–LO5 overall ratings at Week 1 |
| `Post_Assessment_Score` | Number (decimal) | Average of LO1–LO5 overall ratings at Week 12 |
| `LO1_Pre` through `LO5_Pre` | Number (1–4) | Optional — enables per-LO delta charts in Power BI |
| `LO1_Post` through `LO5_Post` | Number (1–4) | Optional |

### Deployment Checklist

- [ ] Distribute in Week 1 orientation — before any project work or team assignments
- [ ] Collect all 30 pre-scores and enter into Student Roster by end of Week 1
- [ ] Re-administer the **identical form** in Week 12 — do not change wording
- [ ] Collect all 30 post-scores and enter before interview week begins
- [ ] Confirm `SkillDelta = Post_Assessment_Score − Pre_Assessment_Score` calculates correctly in Power BI

### What Feeds the Dean's Report vs. the Dashboard

| Data | Goes to |
|---|---|
| Overall LO scores (1–4) | Power BI dashboard (SkillDelta, AACSB charts) |
| Sub-skill grid ratings | Optional per-LO breakdown charts in Power BI |
| Optional open-ended responses | Dean's Report qualitative narrative (via Mini Interview) |

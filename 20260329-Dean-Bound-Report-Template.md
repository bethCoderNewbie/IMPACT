## Section 6: Data Collection Methodology

To ensure this report reflects authentic student experience — beyond formal evaluations — IMPACT implemented a supplemental data collection process this semester **in addition to** Teresa's existing self-evaluations and mentor evaluations.

**Mini Focus Group Process:**
- Team leads conducted brief structured interviews with each team member during the final two weeks of the semester
- Interviews followed a standardized template (developed by [Alex] and IMPACT leadership) covering: technical skill development, leadership growth, career outcomes, and program feedback
- Responses were compiled by team leads and submitted to operations for aggregation

**Dashboard & Visualization:**
- Beth [Last Name] compiled quantitative responses into a Power BI dashboard for real-time visualization
- Sydney [Last Name] and Naya [Last Name] contributed to visual design and layout
- Raw data is stored in SharePoint and available upon request

**Data Governance:**
- All salary and placement data is reported in aggregate; individual student data is not disclosed without consent
- Photo usage is governed by the IMPACT media consent form signed at onboarding

# IMPACT — Mini Focus Group Interview Template
## For Team Leads | End-of-Semester Data Collection

**Purpose:** Capture student growth data, career outcomes, and program highlights for the Dean's Bound Report.
This is supplemental to Teresa's existing self-evaluations and mentor evaluations.
**Administered by:** Team Lead (verbal interview or written form — your discretion)
**Timing:** Final 2 weeks of semester
**Time required:** ~10–15 minutes per student
**Submission:** Submit compiled team responses to Operations by [Deadline Date]

---

## Instructions for Team Leads

1. Schedule a brief 1:1 or small group conversation with each team member, OR share this as a written form.
2. Record responses as specifically as possible — quote the student where you can.
3. For salary/placement data: ask students to share voluntarily; do not pressure. Report in aggregate only.
4. Collect at least one photo per team for the report (project work, team meeting, client visit, etc.). Confirm media consent was signed at onboarding.
5. Submit all responses (and photos) to Operations via [SharePoint folder link] by [Deadline].

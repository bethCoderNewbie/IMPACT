# W. Frank Barton School of Business — Degree Programs

---

## Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

### Accounting *(Also available online)*
- Prepare and analyze financial statements (GAAP/IFRS)
- Apply tax law and complete federal/state tax filings
- Perform auditing and internal control procedures
- Use accounting software (QuickBooks, SAP, Excel)
- Conduct cost and managerial accounting analysis
- Understand business law and regulatory compliance
- Communicate financial data to non-financial stakeholders

### Business Administration *(Also available online)*
- Apply core principles across accounting, finance, marketing, and management
- Analyze business problems and develop strategic solutions
- Lead teams and manage organizational change
- Interpret financial statements and business performance data
- Communicate effectively in written, verbal, and presentation formats
- Apply ethical reasoning to business decisions

### Economics
- Apply microeconomic and macroeconomic theory to real-world problems
- Analyze market structures, pricing, and consumer behavior
- Use econometric methods and statistical software (R, Stata)
- Evaluate fiscal and monetary policy impacts
- Interpret economic data and construct forecasts
- Conduct cost-benefit and regression analysis

### Entrepreneurship
- Develop and validate a business concept or venture
- Write and pitch a business plan and financial projections
- Identify funding sources (bootstrapping, angel, VC)
- Assess market opportunity and competitive landscape
- Manage startup operations and early-stage growth
- Apply design thinking and innovation frameworks

### Finance
- Perform financial modeling, valuation, and forecasting
- Analyze investment opportunities and manage portfolios
- Apply risk management principles and derivatives concepts
- Interpret financial statements and corporate reports
- Use Bloomberg, Excel, and financial databases
- Understand capital markets and corporate finance structure

### Hospitality
- Manage hotel, restaurant, or event operations
- Apply revenue management and yield strategies
- Deliver high-quality guest experience and service recovery
- Oversee food & beverage operations and cost control
- Use property management and point-of-sale systems
- Apply marketing and sales techniques in hospitality contexts

### Human Resource Management *(Also available online)*
- Recruit, select, and onboard employees effectively
- Design compensation, benefits, and performance management systems
- Apply employment law and ensure HR compliance (FLSA, EEOC, FMLA)
- Develop training and organizational development programs
- Manage employee relations and conflict resolution
- Use HRIS platforms (Workday, ADP)

### Information Technology and Management Information Systems
- Analyze and design information systems for business processes
- Manage databases and write basic SQL queries
- Apply IT project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
- Evaluate cybersecurity risks and data governance policies
- Build and interpret business intelligence dashboards
- Bridge communication between technical teams and business stakeholders

### International Business *(Also available online)*
- Analyze global market entry strategies and trade regulations
- Navigate cross-cultural communication and management
- Apply international finance concepts (exchange rates, hedging)
- Understand import/export compliance and logistics
- Evaluate political, economic, and cultural risk in foreign markets
- Develop global marketing and supply chain strategies

### Management *(Also available online)*
- Lead and motivate diverse teams toward organizational goals
- Apply strategic planning and decision-making frameworks
- Manage projects, timelines, and resource allocation
- Analyze organizational behavior and culture
- Resolve conflict and facilitate negotiation
- Evaluate operational processes for efficiency improvements

### Marketing *(Also available online)*
- Conduct market research and consumer behavior analysis
- Develop integrated marketing campaigns across channels
- Build and manage brand strategy and positioning
- Apply pricing, product, place, and promotion (4 Ps) frameworks
- Measure marketing performance through KPIs and analytics
- Create advertising and promotional content

### Marketing — Data-Driven Marketing and Intelligence
- Collect, clean, and analyze customer and campaign data
- Build predictive models for segmentation and targeting
- Use CRM systems and marketing automation platforms
- Interpret A/B test results and attribution reports
- Apply data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI)
- Translate data insights into actionable marketing strategies

### Marketing — Digital Marketing *(Also available online)*
- Plan and execute SEO, SEM, and paid advertising campaigns
- Manage social media channels and content calendars
- Design email marketing campaigns and automation flows
- Analyze web analytics using Google Analytics and similar tools
- Apply conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques
- Develop digital content strategy aligned with business goals

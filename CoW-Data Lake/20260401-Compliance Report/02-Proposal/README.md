## What a Proposal Should Include

1. **Executive Summary** — What problem we are solving and what CoW will receive
2. **Scope of Work** — What is included and explicitly what is not
3. **Approach & Methodology** — How we will conduct the engagement phase by phase
4. **Team & Credentials** — Who will do the work and relevant experience
5. **Timeline** — High-level schedule with key milestones
6. **Pricing** — Total cost, payment schedule, and assumptions
7. **Assumptions & Dependencies** — What we are relying on from the client

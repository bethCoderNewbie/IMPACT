## 06a — Data Governance Review Report

**Output:** `06a-Governance-Review/20260401-DG-Review-Report-v1.md` → `FINAL/`

Covers:
1. Executive Summary
2. Methodology (DAMA-DMBOK v2, NIST SP 800-188, ISO 8000)
3. Current State Assessment
4. Compliance Gap Matrix (10 domains)
5. Industry Benchmarking
6. Recommendations & Prioritization
7. Risk Register
8. Appendix A — Data quality findings (609 issues, 45 files)
9. Appendix B — RACI matrix

**Accepted when:** CoW confirms current-state accuracy, gap matrix is evidence-backed across all 10 domains, and recommendations are actionable with named owners.

---

## 06b — System Requirements Document

**Output:** `06b-System-Requirements/20260401-System-Requirements-v1.md` → `FINAL/`

Covers:
1. Business Context & Objectives
2. Stakeholder Register
3. Functional Requirements (7 domains: ingestion, storage, access, retention, search, reporting, audit)
4. Non-Functional Requirements (performance, security, compliance, scalability)
5. Data Architecture Diagram
6. Integration Requirements (Laserfiche Cloud + existing CoW systems)
7. Acceptance Criteria
8. Open Issues & Decisions Log

**Accepted when:** IT department validates functional requirements, all SLAs are confirmed, and the document is complete enough to share with a vendor or internal project team.

## Revision Policy

- Up to **2 rounds of revisions** per deliverable are included in scope
- Client feedback must be submitted in writing (email or annotated document)
- Revised draft returned within **3 business days** of receiving feedback
- Additional revision rounds require a signed Change Order

# _Internal — Team Only

**Purpose:** Working documents, internal analysis, and team communications that are **not for client distribution**. Everything in this folder stays within the IMPACT consulting team.

> **Access:** Do not share this folder or its contents with CoW or any external party.

---

## Subfolders

| Folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| `Working-Papers/` | In-progress analysis, scratch notes, data explorations, and intermediate calculations that inform but do not appear directly in deliverables |
| `Internal-Analysis/` | Internal assessments, quality checks on our own work, and pre-review drafts before they are ready for client eyes |
| `Team-Communications/` | Internal email threads, meeting notes, and decisions made within the consulting team (not client-facing) |

---

## What Belongs Here

- Draft deliverable versions before they are ready to share with the client
- Internal debate or discussion notes on how to frame a finding
- Working calculations or data pivots used to derive a result
- Peer review comments between team members
- Any document with internal cost, rate, or margin information

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Anything that has been shared with the client (move to the appropriate phase folder)
- Final approved deliverables (those go in `06-Delivery/FINAL/`)
- Signed contracts or client documents (those go in `03-Contract/`)

---

## Naming Convention

```
YYYYMMDD-Description-INTERNAL.md
```

The `-INTERNAL` suffix is a reminder that the file has not been cleared for client sharing.

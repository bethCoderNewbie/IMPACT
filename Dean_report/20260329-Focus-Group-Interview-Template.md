# IMPACT — Mini Focus Group Interview Template
## For Team Leads | End-of-Semester Data Collection

**Purpose:** Capture behavioral evidence of student growth, career outcomes, and program highlights for the Dean's Bound Report.
This is supplemental to Teresa's existing self-evaluations and mentor evaluations.
**Administered by:** Team Lead (verbal interview — record responses as notes, OR share as written form)
**Timing:** Final 2 weeks of semester
**Time required:** ~15 minutes per student
**Submission:** Submit compiled team responses to Operations by [Deadline Date]

> **Design note (2026-03-29):** Modules 1 and 2 now use behavioral evidence prompts instead of self-rating scales. Students describe specific things they did — not how they feel about their skills. This produces more accurate data for the Dean's report and feeds directly into the PM BARS rubric and peer nomination scores.

---

## Instructions for Team Leads

1. Schedule a brief 1:1 with each team member, OR share this as a written form.
2. Record responses as specifically as possible — quote the student where you can. The more specific (named tool, named deliverable, named situation), the better.
3. For salary/placement data: ask students to share voluntarily; do not pressure. Report in aggregate only.
4. Collect at least one photo per team for the report (project work, team meeting, client visit). Confirm media consent was signed at onboarding.
5. Submit all responses (and photos) to Operations via [SharePoint folder link] by [Deadline].

## How Module 1 & 2 Responses Feed the Measurement System

The behavioral evidence collected above maps directly to the 3-lens scoring system:

| Module | Response | Feeds Into |
|---|---|---|
| 1a — Named deliverable + tool | Confirms Lens A deliverable quality score | Lens A (LO2) |
| 1b — PM skills tested | Supports PM BARS item: "flagged blockers proactively" | Lens B (LO3) |
| 2a — Decision made | Supports PM BARS item: "student identified issue PM hadn't raised" | Lens B (LO2) |
| 2b — Disagreement handled | Supports peer nomination: "most reliable/professional" | Lens B (LO3) |
| 2c — Took ownership | Supports PM BARS item: "initiated update without prompting" | Lens B (LO1) |
| 2e — Blocker timing | Directly maps to PM BARS item: "flagged blockers before deadline" | Lens B (LO3) |

Team leads: share your Module 2 notes with the PM before they complete the BARS rubric. It gives them specific evidence to check against.
